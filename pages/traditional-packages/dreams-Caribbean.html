Our Dishes

Costa Rican cuisine embodies the heart and soul of its rich culture, offering a palette of flavors that reflect the nation's biodiversity. From the comforting gallo pinto to the zesty ceviche, each dish tells a story of tradition and natural abundance. As we celebrate this culinary heritage, we are excited to showcase the following dishes that represent the essence of our catering service.

Rice and Beans

Rice and beans are cooked together with coconut and spices, such as achiote, giving it a unique flavor.

  • Price per unit: $10
Cocadas

These are sweets made with grated coconut and sugar, often incorporating other ingredients such as pineapple, guava, or nuts.

  • Price per unit: $7
Caribbean-style Chicken

Caribbean-style chicken is seasoned with local spices and slow-cooked in a mixture of orange juice, lemon, garlic, and other spices until tender and full of flavor.

  • Price per unit: $20
Pan Bon

It is a traditional sweet bread prepared with grated coconut, sugar, coconut milk, and spices. It's a delight to enjoy as a dessert or snack.

  • Price per unit: $5
Patacones with beans

They are made with mashed green plantains and then fried until crispy. They are often served with beans as a side dish or with sauces as a main course.

  • Price per unit: $10
Patty

This is a pastry filled with seasoned meat and wrapped in puff pastry or empanada dough. It is a popular dish throughout the Caribbean region.

  • Price per unit: $10
