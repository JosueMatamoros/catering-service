Our Dishes

Costa Rican cuisine embodies the heart and soul of its rich culture, offering a palette of flavors that reflect the nation's biodiversity. From the comforting gallo pinto to the zesty ceviche, each dish tells a story of tradition and natural abundance. As we celebrate this culinary heritage, we are excited to showcase the following dishes that represent the essence of our catering service.

Pan Sobado

It is a traditional white bread typically made by hand. The main ingredients are wheat flour, water, yeast, salt, sugar, and shortening.

  • Price $2 per serving
Rosquillas

They are sweet and crispy doughnuts, often flavored with anise. Common ingredients include wheat flour, sugar, eggs, butter, anise, and baking powder.

  • Price $3 per serving
Cornbread

This bread is made with cornmeal, giving it a distinctive flavor. Ingredients typically include cornmeal, sugar, butter, eggs, baking powder, and milk.

  • Price $1 per serving
Braided Bread

It is a sweet braided bread that can be filled with fruits, cream, or dulce de leche. The basic ingredients are wheat flour, sugar, yeast, milk, eggs, and butter.

  • Price $1 per serving
Sweet Bread

This sweet bread comes in a variety of shapes and flavors. Common ingredients include wheat flour, sugar, milk, butter, eggs, and yeast.

  • Price $1 per serving
Chiverre Empanadas

Common ingredients for the dough of the empanadas include wheat flour, vegetable shortening, sugar, eggs, baking powder, and salt.

  • Price $4 per serving
